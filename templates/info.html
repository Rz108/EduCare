Understanding Stress and Anxiety

What is Stress?

Stress is a natural reaction to the challenges and demands of life. It can manifest as physical, emotional, or psychological tension. Some stress is normal and can even be helpful in certain situations, but chronic stress can be harmful to your health.

Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of unease, such as worry or fear, that can be mild or severe. It's a common reaction to stress and can become overwhelming if not managed effectively.

Managing Stress and Anxiety

There are various strategies for managing stress and anxiety, including regular exercise, mindfulness and relaxation techniques, seeking support from friends and professionals, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Seeking Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with stress or anxiety that is interfering with daily life, it's important to seek help from a mental health professional. Do inform your teacher too.
